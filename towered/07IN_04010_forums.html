Forums

You have full access to the American Higher Education Alliance network of instructors, administrators, and industry experts. Visit the Forums to discuss leading industry topics and to create some of your own. Go to Forums by selecting Forums in your left navigation menu. Select Enter Forum on the forum tile of your choice to enter a forum space. Meet AHEA Forum Moderators, share your expertise and learn from others in your field.

Create a Forum Topic

Within any forum, you can create a Forum Topic by selecting the New Topic button in the Topics header tile. Name your topic title and enter a question or comment in the post field. Select the Save button to create your new Topic and to begin engaging in discussion within your topic thread.

Respond to Forum Topics

Engage in forum discussion by selecting the View Thread button on the topic of your choosing. Selecting this will guide you to the forum topic discussion where you will see the full topic question or comment, along with all the responses that your peers have added to the discussion. Select the Reply hyperlink on any given message to respond to posts within the forum topic thread.
