Project Planning

Our collaborative platform is designed to guide you through Course and Certificate Development Projects. Create projects from your interests on the profile screen or start a new project on the My Projects screen. While in the project space, complete tasks to advance to milestones. Invite additional partners while you work. Add files and send messages to partners for effective collaboration. Stay tuned as we expand our project offerings to peer reviews, study abroad programs, and more.

Partner Matching

CollaboratED™ partners instructors based on a special matching algorithm and provides partner recommendations. Select the Find Matches button, in the Collaboration Interests tile within your profile, to view prospective partners that you have matched with based on your interest entries.

On the Matches page, you can browse through prospects, view their profiles, send your peers messages, and invite them to projects. To view a match's profile, select their Name or Profile Image.

From your paired partner's profile, you can select the View More button in the Institution Profile tile to visit your prospective partner's institution's profile and learn more about their school. To continue prepare and plan for your project, you can select the Message icon to send a message to your partner or select the Invite button to invite him or her to your upcoming project.
