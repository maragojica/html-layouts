Platform

The InspirED™ platform is designed to support the critical mission and goals of higher education: teaching and learning, institutional effectiveness, student engagement and outcomes, growth strategies, curriculum development, efficiency and cost containment.

InspirED natively combines a professional engagement and faculty development solution, CollaboratED™, with a learning management solution, BlendED™. The solution provides a common workspace for academic leaders and faculty, and includes key networking, collaboration and student learning features. The use of CollaboratED and BlendED results in innovative courses and certificates that engage students in both virtual and physical learning environments.

Add to this a student information system, CRM, analytics, and our student mobile app, and your institution will be InspirED.
