Course Design

Users can modify the format of the course by incorporating collapsing week and topic formats, and drag and drop customization within the course screen. Instructional Designers, Instructors, and LMS Administrators can modify the formatting of a course within the BlendED™ Learning Management System by configuring the course's settings.

Course Home

The BlendED™ Learning Management System had designed courses intentionally to present a seamless experience for Instructors, Students, and Administrators.

To navigate to your course, you can click on My Courses or select your course from the My Courses tile on your Home screen.

The Course Menu allows users to navigate through the Badges, Competencies, Participants, and Grade Report sections of the Course.

Instructors can select the TURN EDITING ON button to modify the blocks on the Course screen.
