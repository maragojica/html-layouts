Initial Sign In

To get started in InspirED, you'll first need to confirm your account.

1. Click the link in your welcome email to access the "Sign In Screen".

2. Enter your email address and the password provided in your email.

3. Enter and confirm your new password and click Confirm.

Tip: Passwords must contain at least 1 upper case letter, 1 lower case letter, 1 number, and 1 special character (######).

4. Enter your email address and newly created password and click Sign In.
