Forgot Password

If you need to sign in but don't remember your password, follow these steps:

1. On the "Sign In Screen" click Forgot Password.

2. Enter your institutional email and click Send Password Reset Link.

3. A message will display at the top of the screen: The recovery process has started. If your email exists in our records, you should receive an email with the recovery instructions. From your email, click Reset Password.

4. Enter a new password into the New Password and Confirm Password fields and click Reset.

5. The "Sign In Screen" will load with a message at the top of the screen: You have successfully updated your password.

6. Enter your email and newly created password and select Sign In.
