Home Screen

Your "Home Screen" contains tiles to provide easy access to everything the platform has to offer. The "Home Screen" provides the most relevant information from the moment you enter the platform helping you navigate through your daily work, achieve your daily and long-term goals, and is tailored to your role. The tiles below are available on your "Home Screen".

Announcements

Announcements are updated on a frequent basis to give you information on the latest events and opportunities within the educational community. You can view each announcement as it scrolls or use the numbered buttons to view.

My Projects

The My Projects Tile keeps open projects accessible by displaying key pieces of information on your "Home Screen". The fields shown in this tile include:

  • Project Title
  • Start and End
  • Type
  • Organizer
  • Files
  • Partners
  • Status

Recommended Partners

The Recommended Partners Tile displays other users who share commonalities or interests. To find out more about a Recommended Partner, click the user's name to be brought to the selected user's "Profile Screen."

Resources

Helpful guidance and tutorials are only a click away from the "Home Screen". Click Resources to view the Resources Screen. Or you can click any of the listed links, including Getting Started, Best Practices, Templates, and Tutorials, to go directly to that section.

Communicate Now

As you make connections throughout the platform, your new connections will appear in the Communicate Now Tile. To send a message to a user, click the icon. Clicking the icon will let you compose and send an email to that user.

"Contacts" are people in your network, but with whom you don't necessarily have a working relationship with yet. "Connections" represent people you have some level of relationship with, such as a project partner or colleague from your department or institution.

A Contact may be promoted up to a Connection once a good working relationship has been established, and Connections may in turn be promoted to Favorites for quick access to those you are most likely to be communicating with on a frequent basis.

Calendar

Not only does the Calendar Tile give you a quick snapshot of upcoming dates, by clicking My Calendar, you will be brought to the "Calendar Screen". In addition, a dropdown menu allows you to access events for specific projects.

Please note: a number of tiles included on the Home Screen are grayed-out. These tiles will be active in a future release.
