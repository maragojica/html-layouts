Top Navigation

The Top Navigation Menu provides access to Notifications and Messages. In addition, clicking your picture displays a menu with quick links to your "My Profile Screen", Settings, and Sign Out.

Badges

In a future release, your badges earned within the system will display by clicking this icon.

Notifications

The number displayed in the orange circle on Notifications shows the number of unread notifications waiting for you. Access the system notifications by clicking the Notifications icon to easily stay up-to-date with changes including:

  • Potential partner invitations that have been accepted or declined.
  • Upcoming, newly assigned, or completed project tasks.
  • Project milestones achieved.

Messages

Similar to Notifications, the Message icon also displays the number of unread messages. When the Message icon is clicked, a dropdown shows a preview of new messages, which can be clicked to open the conversation on the "Messages Screen".

Profile Picture

Your profile picture, or placeholder, will also be displayed in the Top Menu with a greeting. By clicking your profile picture or "Hello [your name] text, a dropdown menu shows quick links that will take you to:

  • "My Profile Screen"
  • Settings
  • Sign Out

Breadcrumbs

Directly below the Top Navigation icons, the name of your current location within the platform will display and links to navigate to previous pages within the platform hierarchy are available.

Support

You can click Support Documentation to be directed to the support area. If you need to communicate with our technical support team, click Contact Support.
