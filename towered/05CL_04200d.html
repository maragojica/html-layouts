Name: Cross-Cultural Business Management Certification

Description

This project is to design a graduate-level certificate in cross-cultural business communications. Rather than just 'reading' about common practices, students will experience them by studying in a global classroom and working through a multinational corporation case study on management and communication strategies.

Certificate Summary
Certificate Name: Cross-Cultural Business Communication Certificate
Certificate Number: BUS 600 Cert
Prerequisites: None
Units: 2 CEUs
Certificate Level: Graduate
Time Commitment: 1 Week
Instructor 1 Qualifications: Earned Master's Degree in Business Administration and 5 - 10 years of experience in private sector business management
Instructor 2 Qualifications: Earned Doctorate in Social Sciences (Sociology, Anthropology, Economics)
Certificate Description: This certificate course will provide you with the opportunity to learn and practice communication strategies for cross-cultural business settings. You will have a real multicultural experience by working with students in another country and culture. Using technology, you will work in cross-cultural teams to explore and analyze management and communication strategies of a multi-national corporation.
Certificate Topics: 1. Cultures and Languages
2. Gender, Religion, and Class
3. Family and Work-Life
Certificate Objectives: 1. Identify cultural differences in verbal and non-verbal communication
2. Communicate in a business setting with a person from a different culture, language, or both
Learning Environments: 1. Synchronous Video Lecture
2. Independent Assignments
3. Group Assignments
Certificate Standards: International Society for Communication Standards (ISCS) - https://www.iscs.io
