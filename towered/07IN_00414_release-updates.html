InspirED™ 2.0

InspirED™ 2.0 includes an update to the infrastructure of the platform as well as the following new features:

Account Management

  • Updated privacy policy and terms of service are accessible on the sign-in screen and within platform settings
  • Green indicators on profile images allow you to view the online status of your connections
  • Change your password within your settings

Profile

  • Change your profile image to your preferred photo
  • Update your profile with your communication preferences, memberships, certifications, field of study, areas of expertise, and CV
  • View faculty match profiles based on your customized project interests

Institution Profiles

  • View institution profiles within the platform to view information about academic institutions, their faculty, and their student body.

Announcements

  • View announcements from the American Higher Education Alliance (AHEA) within the platform from your home screen

Resources

  • Utilize AHEA's professionally-constructed templates to support curriculum development, campus programs, and strategic initiatives
  • Gain quick tips and instructions on using the InspirED™ platform
  • Discover techniques and best practices that will help manage the academic lifecycle, collaborate with peers, and engage constituents

Calendar

  • Manage your schedule with a calendar that allows you to invite others, add locations, add notes, and set up recurring events
  • Collaboration is made easier with a calendar that displays task due dates from your projects with peers

Messages and Connections

  • Quickly view your most recent messages from anywhere within the platform
  • Compose and respond to messages with an integrated, thread-style inbox.
  • View, email, or message your connections from your InspirED™ home

Faculty Forums

  • Browse the American Higher Education Alliance's forum spaces and engage with forum moderators and global educators
  • Create topics and post responses to the forum topics from our growing forum space

CollaboratED™

CollaboratED™ matches global educators and provides partner recommendations. The project workspace is empowered with discussion and file management capabilities that make collaboration fruitful. Educators can manage projects with ease using CollaboratED™. Users can customize project milestones, collaborate in groups, and view a portfolio of completed projects.

Project Management

  • Create interests to formalize your desire to work with other educators on a project and turn your interest into an achievement
  • Limit your match to your department, institution, or region; or match with our network of faculty from academic institutions around the world
  • Gain partner recommendations based on your project interests
  • Access an integrated calendar that manages calendars from your project to the classroom
  • Manage files within project workspaces for effective planning and collaboration
  • Invite partners before you start your project and while you work to ensure that you have the valuable project outcomes
  • Monitor your project's progress and gain updates as your team reaches project milestones 

Course Development

  • Create interests that are specifically catered towards course development
  • Build a course, or create a co-taught experience for your students, using CollaboratED™'s course development project workflow and workspace
  • Complete your project with end products to share with your academic institutions for course approval and delivery

Certificate Program Development

  • Create interests that outline details about the certificate program you would like to develop
  • Create a certificate program with peers from your region or from around the globe

InspirED™ 2.1

InspirED™ 2.1 introduces the following new features:

Account Management

  • An updated person record allows for platform administrators to opt certain platform users to not appear in CollaboratED™ match results

Profile

  • Student profiles are customized to include fields appropriate for student users

Announcements

  • The American Higher Education Alliance (AHEA) can now reorder announcements displayed in the platform with a reordering feature for announcement management

Resources

  • The American Higher Education Alliance (AHEA) can now reorder resources displayed in the platform with a reordering feature for resource management

Notifications

  • AHEA Institutional members can now receive notifications from our BlendED™ Learning Management System through the InspirED™ by selecting the notifications icon on your top navigation menu
  • Mark all messages as read by selecting a Mark All Read button in your top navigation menu

User Role Management

  • Student role configuration is currently available in InspirED™. Students can now access core features in InspirED™ as well as courses in our BlendED™ Learning Management System with AHEA’s Institutional Membership.

CollaboratED™

CollaboratED™ matches global educators and provides partner recommendations. The project workspace is empowered with discussion and file management capabilities that make collaboration fruitful. Educators can manage projects with ease using CollaboratED™. Users can customize project milestones, collaborate in groups, and view a portfolio of completed projects.

File Management

  • Restore deleted files placed in the Recycle Bin in the Project workspaces

BlendED™

Instructors can create dynamic learning experiences with discipline-specific activities, resources, and tools with our BlendED™ Learning Management System. By configuring student outcomes, instructors can measure their students’ mastery of skills and concepts. Users can modify the format of the course design by incorporating collapsing week and topic formats, with additional drag and drop customization within the course screen.

Course Administration

  • Create course shells and courses with our Learning Management System
  • Organize courses with course categories
  • Manage instructor and student enrollment
  • Import, restore, and backup courses individually and en mass
  • Perform automatic course backups
  • Manage classroom access for various user roles and individuals

Course Design

  • Modify course formats by incorporating collapsing week and topic formats with drag and drop customization within the course screen
  • Configure course settings including general settings, description, format, appearance, files, completion tracking, tags, groups, and role-naming options
  • Customize the Course front page with blocks that add additional course content to the course screen

Activities and Resources

  • Easily add, modify, and remove course assignments and activities
  • Create quizzes with randomized question order, varying question types, time limits, single-user and group overrides, and more
  • Import course activities and resources from external files
  • Create and complete math and engineering problems from our rich text editor tool
  • Use TeX to build text-based equations
  • Configure student outcomes in correlation with course activities to assess competency achievement
  • Students can submit assignment drafts, resubmit assignments, and easily manage deadlines with an integrated course activity Calendar

Grading and Attendance

  • Access course gradebook that allows effective monitoring and management of student grades and outcomes
  • Manage attendance by topic, week, meeting session, or activity
  • Restrict access to assignment solutions and feedback
  • Manage Grade Report settings, grade scales, grade letters, grade aggregation, and grade categories
  • View student grades in real-time
  • Overwrite grades
  • Set date and time parameters for assignment access
  • View and sort student submissions by course section
  • Import and export grades
  • Calculate grades based on weighted averages
  • Access and manage a question bank
  • Utilize an outcome importer to adopt outcomes to your current set of courses

Course File Management

  • Share and upload images, links, documents, video, and audio within the course
  • Add multiple files and file types to course activities and resources
  • Drag and drop files from your device to activities and resources within courses

Course Multimedia

  • View and download recorded and embedded video and audio files

Course Calendar

  • View Course Activities on an integrated Calendar within courses, on the InspirED™ Home, and within the InspirED™ Calendar
