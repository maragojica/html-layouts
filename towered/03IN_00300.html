  • Welcome to InspirED ™

    The InspirED™ platform was created to support your lifelong academic journey. In this release, we are introducing our Learning Management System, BlendED™. Explore the platform by reviewing your home tiles and left navigation menu items.

  • Monitor your Announcements

    At the top of your InspirED™ Home, your Announcements tile displays scrolling messages that will keep you informed on happenings at your institution and within the platform. Return to your Home screen regularly to stay informed of new announcements.

  • Stay Up-to-Date with My Calendar.

    Your InspirED™ Calendar collects and showcases information about your course assignments, activities, and due dates to keep you on track with your academic goals. Synchronize your calendar with your Google or Microsoft account to manage your time more effectively.

  • Access Classes with My Courses.

    Your Courses are always within reach with your My Courses Home tile and screen. On the InspirED™ Home, gain a quick snapshot of classes you are currently enrolled in and your progress in your classes. Select the My Courses in the Left Navigation menu, to view all courses and to enter individual courses you are taking.

  • Navigate through the Course Menu.

    Navigation is made easy within your BlendED™ courses with a Course Menu that directs you through Course Content, Assignments, Grades, Classmates, Competencies, and Badges. After selecting your course, use the Course Menu at the top of your screen to visit various sections effortlessly.

  • Reference the Starter Guide

    Return to the Starter Guide whenever you need a reminder of the latest features in the InspirED platform. Go to Help Menu in the Top Navigation and select Starter Guide to view these tips any time while browsing through the platform.
