5 Cookies

The Platform may utilize "cookies" and other tracking technologies like pixel tags and web beacons (often called "smart cookies") to improve the functionality of the Platform. Cookies are text files that are sent by servers to web browsers and stored on your computer. They tell us which parts of the Platform you've visited so Tower can alert you to software compatibility issues and save your preferences for future visits to the Platform. Web beacons and pixel tags are images embedded in a webpage or e-mail for the purpose of measuring and analyzing usage and activity. We, or third-party service providers acting on our behalf, may use web beacons and pixel tags to help us analyze usage and improve our functionality. Some cookies and other technologies may serve to recall Personal Data previously provided by a user.

We use information collected from cookies and other technologies to improve your user experience and the overall quality of our services. We use cookies to help us identify and track visitors, their usage of the Platform, and their access preferences. We may use your Personal Data to see which web pages you visit at our websites, which website you visited before coming to our website, and where you go after you leave our website. We can then develop statistics that help us understand how our visitors use our websites and how to improve them. Visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using the Platform; provided, however, that Tower disclaims, and you hereby waive, any claim or liability that may arise due to your partial or incomplete access to the content of any of the Platform as a result thereof.

10 Your Data Privacy Rights

Under certain data privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation for users in the European Union, you may have the right to:

  • Right to Access: The right to be provided with a copy of your Personal Data (the right of access).
  • Right to Rectification: The right to require us to correct any mistakes in your Personal Data.
  • Right to be Forgotten: The right to request, in certain situations, deletion of your Personal Data.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: The right to require us to restrict processing of your Personal Data in certain circumstances, e.g. if you contest the accuracy of the data.
  • Right to Data Portability: The right to receive, in certain situations, the Personal Data you provided to us, in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and/or transmit that data to a third party.
  • Right to Object: The right to object (i) at any time to your Personal Data being processed for direct marketing (including profiling), where applicable; or (ii) in certain other situations to our continued processing of your Personal Data, e.g., processing carried out for the purpose of our legitimate interests.
  • Right Not to be Subject to Automated Individual Decision-Making: The right not to be subject to a decision based solely on automated processing (including profiling) that produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you.
