Project Planning

Our collaborative platform is designed to guide you through Course and Certificate Development Projects. Create projects from your interests on the profile screen or start a new project on the My Projects screen. While in the project space, complete tasks to advance to milestones. Invite additional partners while you work. Add files and send messages to partners for effective collaboration. Stay tuned as we expand our project offerings to peer reviews, study abroad programs, and more.

Create an Interest

Every project is sparked by an interest. Add interests to your profile to share with compatible matches and peers. Interests are used to aggregate matches within the platform and enable harmonious collaboration.

To create an interest, visit your Profile by selecting My Profile in your Top Navigation menu. Scroll down to the Collaboration Interests tile and select the Add button.

First, in the Project Type field, select the type of project that you are interested in achieving. Describe your project, in the Description field, and indicate the language you would prefer to communicate in while collaborating, in the Preferred Language field. Select the Delivery Method of your course or certificate. The Collaboration Type specifies whether you are interested in collaborating in a project within your department, your institution, or our region, or global networks. Select the Regions within our network that you are interested in finding a partner in. Additionally, you can specify the specific Subject Areas that your project covers so that we can find you a match that specializes in your selected areas. From the Program Length field, select the option that best describes the academic calendar at your institution. Enter other relevant project information requested such as Credits, Time Commitment, Program Level, Certificate Level, and the anticipated Start and End Date of your project. Select the Save button to save your interest to your profile.
