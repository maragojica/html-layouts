Project Milestones

CollaboratED™ guides educators through a set of milestones to complete a specific project type. By completing required tasks within a given milestone, project partners can successfully complete milestones to complete their project and access end products to help support the delivery of their course or certification program.

The Milestone Bar and the Progress Percentage icon indicate how the project team is advancing towards project completion. The percentage of completion is based on required tasks completed. The Milestone Bar acts as an indicator of how far your team is on your project and as a navigation bar to assist with navigating within the project space to previously achieved milestones.

When all required tasks are complete in the Task Overview tile, the Complete Milestone button will change color from grey to green. This means that the team can advance to the next milestone of their project by selecting the Complete Milestone button.

Please Note: Optional tasks can be added by selecting the Add button in the Task Overview tile. These optional tasks are not required to advance to the next milestone.
